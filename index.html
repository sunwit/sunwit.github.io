Brief Bio

I am a Ph.D. student in Department of Computer Science and Engineering,The Ohio State University. Advised by Prof. Kannan Srinivasan ,I am interested in wireless networking. I received M.S. in computer science from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) . In SJTU, I was supervised by Prof. Yanmin Zhu. I received my B.S. in computer science from Anhui Science and Technology University.

Research Interests

My research interests are in the area of networked system, with a focus on developing the algorithms and technologies for resouce allocation in mobile crowdsourcing, RFID and powerline communication. My research draws on tools from machine learning, signal processing and optimization.
